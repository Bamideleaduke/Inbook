OUR STORY

Business can be demanding, but without good insight, a business is on the brink of failure. Inbook was developed by a group of individuals who witnessed the failure of countless Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) in Nigeria as a result of their inability to maintain their financial records, which hampered their capacity to obtain loans from financial institutions. We also discovered that these SMEs' owners wasted time on administrative duties on the creation of invoices and keeping track of sales manually. This is the motivation behind the creation of Inbook. Inbook was designed to be a one-stop website for all bookkeeping, invoicing, and inventory management needs, saving businesses time on administrative tasks. Inbook provides access to data-driven insights tailored to the firm's needs, as well as automatic invoice creation.

OUR VISION

An automated platform for Invoice creation and documentation system for business owners. Inbook provides bookkeeping and e-invoice solutions that help business owners manage their inventory and track records of sales overtime automatically.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to aid the sustainability of SMEs in the long run and also help them have adequate insight into their operations, which can help them grow their business, access loans from financial institutions, and attract investors .

WHY CHOOSE US

Inbook is committed to assisting SMEs in achieving comprehensive financial records from sales and profits, as well as providing a solid framework to secure your business's long-term growth.
